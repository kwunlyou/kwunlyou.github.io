Physical Based Animation

Kun Liu

INRIA, France


Abstract:

In the project, sparse temporal tracking data is acquired by using mutiple cameras. In order to reconstruct the object animation, a physical deformable model is introduced. The finite element method is also applied to solve the PDE (partial differential equation) produced by the model.
